We are Refugee Info Bus. We are volunteers providing free wifi, legal information and language lessons to refugees.

We work alongside many organisations at l’Auberge des Migrants in Calais. All of these organisations are run by volunteers.

 

Help Refugees provide clothes, shoes, blankets, sleeping bags and hygiene items, and work with refugees around the world.

RCK cook and serve thousands of hot meals every day to refugees living in Calais and Dunkirk.

Utopia 56 distribute hot food, tea, and material aid in smaller locations around Calais.

RYS provides mobile support for children, young people and unaccompanied minors in Northern France, and offers legal support, information, wifi, phone charging and games.
